About

The history of the Movers

First and only USA B2B movers platform to share trucks and loads
The Moving industry trucks-loads control is still done same as 100 years ago, dispatchers need to do everything manually, and the moving companies are not connected, since 2015 we saw the urgent need of this industry to make the big progress and jump into the high-tech era, we created large groups of moving companies, and in 2020 we build this amazing tool for all moving companies to work together and share trucks and loads available nationwide. This platform will help companies to maximize on-road efficiency and improve their shipment delivery services and save costs in all aspects of the operation.

We are entrepreneurs form the moving area that want to make our and other companies moving easier
